Make a Thankful Tree

      A branch, some construction paper leaves, and a few things to be thankful for: that’s all you need to make this thankful tree! Have the whole family add leaves with the things they’re thankful for. Use it as cute fall décor or as a centerpiece for your Thanksgiving table.
  1. First Step is to cut out some paper leaves and write what you are thankful for on them.
  2. Second step is to find a Tree or branch and punch a hole onto the cut out leaf papers
  3. Third Step is to place you leaves onto the branch and your done

Buttermilk Mashed Potatoes

Buttery, fluffy mashed potatoes are an essential part of Thanksgiving. Russet potatoes make light mashed potatoes, to play better with the rest of the rich holiday dishes on the table. (You can use Yukon Golds instead for a richer, creamier dish.) Buttermilk also keeps the potatoes light, but you can swap in sour cream, crème fraîche or heavy cream, if you like.
